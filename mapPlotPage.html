Food Prices per Region

Foodprices are dependent on a lot of variables. Variables with a larger impact like production, location, time of year etc. For foodsecurity it is important to predict the availability of food and thus the price of food. Before any predictions could be made about foodprices we need clarification about why food prices fluctuate.

On this page we discuss the location of food and it's price. A crop needs ideal climatic conditions for it's growth. So it is logic which explains that climatic conditions influence the price of food. Climatic conditions differ from place to place but doesn't stop at country borders so prices should show some fluctuation per region. This is why we expect a positive correlation in food products and there region.
