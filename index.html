Food Prices

Food is one of the most basic necessities. It has already been shown in previous studies that the price of food directly influences what food we buy. This website provides a visual representation of the global food prices database, which includes food price data for different products from 76 countries, divided in more than 1500 markets, with some countries having recordings from as early as 1992.
